Disinfecting Indoor Air Against Diseases Such as COVID-19 and TB in Cities in the Indian Subcontinent

Project details

Air purifier solution to tackle indoor airborne diseases in India

Nobody wants any pandemic like Covid-19 to happen again. Nearly half of the Indian population is susceptible to TB. The world is facing challenges in tackling such airborne diseases. The indoor confined space is one of the reasons for the spread of such viruses. The present effort is to design and implement a new air purifier system for heavy pollution settings situations in India and the Indian subcontinents.
